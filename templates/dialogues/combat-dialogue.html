Combat Actions

Aim: Required for Ranged attacking at medium+. Gives +3 to hit at Short- range. On second use gain +3 to hit.

Full Defense: +2 Defense until next turn. Cost 1 initiative

Delay: Delaying action till later in the round costs 2 initative.

Movement Actions

Rush: Roll (Dex + Athletics) vs enemy at Short range, on success if the enemy moves away move 1 range bad towards them

Disengage: Roll (Dex + Dodge) vs enemy (Dex + Athletics) to move out of their range band, if they move towards you on their next turn you may reflexivly move away.

Rise from prone: If enemy is within close range requires (Dex + Dodge) vs Diff 2 action

Take Cover: Player rolls (Dex + Dodge) with difficulty depending on terrain to find cover.

Withdraw: Can only be used at Medium+ range, Difficulty 1 Goal 10 interval 1 turn extended action. Lose 10 initative per turn. If at Extreme range exit combat.

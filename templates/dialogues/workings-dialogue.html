Sorcerous Workings

Make an extended (Intelligence + Occult) roll, difficulty set by finesse, goal number set by ambition, base interval of one week.

After completion of the project the sorcerer spends XP. If a sorcerous working is destroyed or lost the sorcerer gains the XP back at the end of the story.

Ambition XP Cost
One 2xp
Two 4xp
Three 8xp

Ambition

The power of the working. Has 3 circles of power Terrestrial, Celestial, and Solar and ratings 1-3 in each.

Ambition Goal Number
Terrestrial 1 5
Terrestrial 2 10
Terrestrial 3 20
Celestial 1 25
Celestial 2 30
Celestial 3 35
Solar 1 40
Solar 2 50
Solar 3 75

Finesse

Sets how much control the sorcerer has over how the working acomplishes its purpose. Finesse sets the difficult of the roll 1, 3, or 5

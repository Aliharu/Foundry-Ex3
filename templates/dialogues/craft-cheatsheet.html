Craft XP

Completing a basic project gives the crafter 2 Silver XP per Craft Objective completed, if the crafter rolled 3+ sucesses above the difficulty they gain 3 silver XP per objective instead.

Completing a major project costs 10 Silver XP gives the crafter 1 Silver XP and 2 Gold XP per Craft Objective completed, if the crafter rolled 3+ sucesses above the difficulty they gains 1 Silver XP and 3 gold XP per objective instead.

Completing a superior project costs 10 Gold XP per roll and if the craft achieved at least 1 objective it gives the crafter White XP equal to [Artifact Rating]x2 + 1, so forging an 3 Dot Artifact gives 7 White XP. For every unused interval remaining in the project, the crafter gains (Artifact rating x 2) gold XP.

Completing a legendary project costs 10 White XP and if the craft achieved at least 1 objective it gives the crafter 10 White XP and the crafter rolls a craft roll with a free full excellency. The craft gains 1 Gold XP per success and 1 Silver XP for every dice that wasn't a success.

Craft Objectives

Another character gains or strengthens an Intimacy toward the crafter.

Produces a clear in-game benefit for the crafter.

Upholds, furthers, or protects one of the crafters intimacies.

End of Story Bonuses

3 Silver XP for every different craft with 3+ dots used to complete a project during the story.

5 Silver XP for every different craft with 5 dots used to complete a project during the story.

Five gold XP for each artifact made by the crafter during the story.

Repairs

Repairing a basic project gives 1 silver XP, 1 Gold XP for a major project, (Artifact Rating - 1) white XP for a superior project, and none for a legendary project.

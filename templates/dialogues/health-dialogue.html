Crippling Wounds

Once per story take a crippling injury instead of damage. Must take at least 2 health levels of damage to take an injury.

Health Levels Example Wound
1-2 Lose An eye, some fingers, a foot
3-4 Blinding, lose hand, maim organs
5+ Lose Limb

Healing Times (Aggravated same as Lethal)

Health Time
Bashing -0 1 Hour
Lethal -0 1 Day
Bashing -1 12 Hours
Lethal -1 2 Days
Bashing -2 One Day
Lethal -2 Three Days
Bashing -4 Two Days
Lethal -4 5 Days
Incap Depends, For bashing typically end of scene.

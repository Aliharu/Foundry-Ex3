Combat Tracker

-This module has integration with Foundry's initiative tracker

-Targeting tokens with withering attacks will alter initiative in the tracker (Double Right Click for players or use the bullseye for GMs)

-Onsluaght penalties are auto calculated on attacks and removed on the actors turn

-New rounds will restore motes

Characters

-Willpower is added automatically from level 2 and 3 stunts.

-Ranged/Thrown/Seige Weapons accuracy is calculated based on range.

-Light/Heavy Cover, Prone, and Grapple/Grappled conditions automatically add penalties/bonuses on attacks and defense. Grapple penalty assumes non 2-handed weapons.

NPCs

-Size bonus is added automatically to health. Battlegroup stats are not added to other rolls currently
